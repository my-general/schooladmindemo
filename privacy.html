Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Read below how we collect and use your information.

1. Information We Collect
We may collect personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and message when you fill out admission or contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used solely for communication, admission processing, or responding to your queries. We do not sell or share your data with any third party.

3. Data Security
We use industry-standard security practices to keep your data safe, including secure form submissions and encrypted data storage (if applicable).

4. Cookies
Our website may use cookies to improve user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

5. Embedded Content
Videos from YouTube or maps from Google may collect data about you, use cookies, or track your interaction. These are governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about our privacy policy, contact us at contact@myschool.edu.in.
